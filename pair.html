Pair Rust+ with RustWire

Important: Use /rustplus connect <IP>:<appPort> in a server text channel (not DM).

  1. Add the bot: Add Bot
  2. Run: /rustplus connect <IP>:<appPort> (e.g. 136.243.70.123:28112)
  3. Approve the request in the Rust+ app on your phone.
