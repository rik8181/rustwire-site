Pair Rust+ with RustWire

  1. Add RustWire to your Discord: Click Add Bot.
  2. Run the command in your server (not DM):
    /rustplus connect <IP>:<appPort>
    Example: /rustplus connect 136.243.70.123:28112
  3. Approve in Rust+ on your phone:
    Open Rust+ → Devices → tap your server → approve the new connection.
    You’ll see a confirmation in Discord.
